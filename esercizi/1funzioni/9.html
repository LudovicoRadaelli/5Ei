Esercizio 1


Calcolare le seguenti derivate:


  1. \(\left(\dfrac{4ln\left(x\right)}{x^2}\right)^{'}\)

  2. \(\left(sin\left(x\right) \cdot cos\left(x\right) +3\right)^{'}\)

  3. \(\left(\dfrac{3 -x}{4 + x + 3x^2}\right)^{'}\)

  4. \(\left[\left(arccos\left(x\right)\right)^3 - 4\right]^{'}\)

  5. \(\left(sin\left(5x^5 -3x +1\right)\right)^{'}\)

  6. \(\left(\sqrt{x-1} \cdot \left(4x^3 -2x^2\right)\right)^{'}\)

  7. \(\left(ln\left(\dfrac{1}{x^2}\right)\right)^{'}\)

  8. \(\left(\dfrac{ln\left(x^3 -1\right)}{x^3 -1}\right)^{'}\)

Soluzione


  1. \(\dfrac{4 - 8\,ln(x)}{x^3}\)

  2. \(cos^2(x)\ - sin^2(x)\)

  3. \(\dfrac{3x^2 -18x -7}{(3x^2 + x + 4)^2}\)

  4. \(-\dfrac{3\,arccos^2(x)}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}}\)

  5. \((25x^4 -3) \, cos(5x^5 -3x + 1)\)

  6. \(\dfrac{x\,(14x^2 - 17x + 4)}{\sqrt{x - 1}}\)

  7. \(-\dfrac{2}{x}\)

  8. \(\dfrac{3x^2 \, \left(ln\left(x^3 -1\right)- 1\right)}{(x^3 -1)^2}\)
